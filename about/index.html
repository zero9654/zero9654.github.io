Hi, there!


I am a UX Designer who loves challenging existing design concepts and pushing boundaries to explore new possibilities on digital products and services. I am passionate about UX/UI design, service design, and graphic design. I am also very interested in design topics such as web accessibility, haptics and collaborative interactions.

In particular, I am writing my master thesis - Web Accessibility Evaluation for Fairdata website at CSC-IT Center For Science. Fairdata website is required to comply with WCAG 2.1 AA level. To identify the potential accessibility issues, I get familiar with various automated evaluation tools and screen readers like Voiceover and NVDA. This thesis project will be completed in September.

I am a double master degree in Human-Computer Interaction and Design; minor in entrepreneurship and innovation. My first year was at Aalto University, and the second year was at University of Paris-Saclay. During my studies, I have involved many design projects with local companies in both Finland and France, such as Posti, HSL, Kide science, Arkhn, etc. These projects range from UX design, service design, usability evaluation, business plan, and business analysis.

I believe that good design not only brings wonderful experience to users but also benefits commerce and more aspects in the world. Currently, I am looking for a UX/Service Design full-time position.
