Rover App

Rover is a collaborative travel planner web application. It is a canvas for creating and managing travel routine via collaboration.

Target user: Mainly target for young people who have trouble with planning trips before and during trips as the main user group.

Main functions: A web application enables users to plan, manage and share the travel plan effectively.

  • Plan: All places of interest are available on the visual map. Users are free to go through those points, know more details, then add these locations to the plan. And users are free to increase or decrease the trip length.
  • Manage: All added locations can be deleted or moved to another time slot or day.
  • Share: The plan owner can share the travel plan with other users. All users can view and modify the plan simultaneously. Also, the plan owner can change the sharing settings.

All interfaces are designed by Figma. Here is the link. Feel free to visit.

Design Philosophy

Since the map itself contains large amount of information already, I keep the simplicity of the interface: less is more. This is to decrease the possible overwhelming feeling.
