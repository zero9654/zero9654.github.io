Defining Problems

  1. Reachability of Customer Service
    It is hard to contact customer service, especailly for consumer customers. If we go through Posti’s website, contact details behind minimum 4 pages and there is no method to contact the customer service for free.

  2. Pick-up Points Fill Up
    Posti pickup points and Smartpost lockers are full during busy seasons. Inevitably, parcels are redirected to locations far from the original delivery location. However, the information about change in delivery location doesn’t work efficiently.

  3. Not All Parcels Can Be Ordered to SmartPosts
    Parcels ordered from different online stores are delivered to different pickup points. Even when a customer has set a smart locker as his pickup point, not all online stores deliver to that smart locker (eg. Zalando).

  4. Barriers for Sending Packages through SmartPost
    Payment is not possible through SmartPost, only through the OmaPosti App or the Posti office desk. Customers who are unfamiliar with web services and with no nearby office will not be able to send the package immediately.
