Divorce Counselling for Human-Robot Marriages

This project came from a course – Design for Transhuman systems. We were asked to design a product or a service based on a topic or area of interest in Tranhumanism.

We have four team members in total. So, we started from individual brainstorming in 5 minutes. We tried to write down as many interesting topics as possible, then we gathered them together and chose those favourite one.

Among those hot topics, we felt the human-robot marriage is rather unique, especailly it is about divorce counselling service for humna-robot marriages. It is novel and worth thinking deeper. Thus, we decided to take this idea further.

We thought about opportunities and problems towards this divorce counselling.

The Concept

The divorce counseling service is linked to robotics: advanced mechatronics, speech recognition, machine vision, imitating/simulating emotions.

We are the agency which make sure human is 100% certain to get divorce with his/her robot. We have marriage counselling to evaluate the condition and process the divorce procedure.

The service is connected to a state where machines can be seen as equals/personally valuable. Humans as a species that create their dream partners. Due to designed emotional connection, “parting ways” with a robot would be a significant moment.

In addition, the service not only provides a decent ceremony to end a relationship, but also handles robot ‘funeral affairs’ eg. taking care of memory storage. Also, we assist human beings return to a traditional human-to-human relationship and help themselves adapt to a new life without a robot partner.

The key value of our services reflects the way of regarding robots as valuable and/or equal. We would like to show different points of view to death. Most importantly, we introduce a culture of relationship-ending ceremonies expanding to human-human relationships.
